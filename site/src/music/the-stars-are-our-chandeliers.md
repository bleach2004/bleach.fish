the stars are our chandeliers
two sharpened swords pierced your ears
your gown as dark as the skies
followed my knees as we danced the night away

your wrist next to mine
as if our heartbeats were to always rhyme
since the very beginning of time
i’ve been waiting and waiting for a day to hold your hand once again

our bodies mirrored, we’re made of sand
how delightful it would be
if only time could stop and it would forever be us
dancing the night away
with all the eyes on us
all the eyes on we
our hands locked in place
alone in the middle of the street

won’t you stay with me
until there’s nothing left to say
and our bodies weave like rivers meet
i’ve been counting down the days

through the trials and the pain
these stormy clouds shall drift away
so honey i’ll be waiting here
because without you
nothing feels the same
feels the same
