the days behind me haven't been kind
days i used to carry like a journal
and wear them like a tender bruise

still, moments ago gratitude washed over me 
reminders of fluorescence,
replaced by a dawning office window
looking down on my car below
an anchor carrying contrast

a human feeling unfamiliar
