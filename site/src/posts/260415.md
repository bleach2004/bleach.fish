scabbing of the face and flaking of the skin 
i could close my eyes to get back on that train
but without witness i'll just swallow my reckoning

the hum of the carriage fuels hazy nostalgia
the scariest part of me, the part i left mangled to shit

i carefully pick out the pieces to continue the drag
for the ramshackle man
wearing his remains

yet i flinch to the left
a grimy pane speaks horrors 
no mirror could ever reflect
